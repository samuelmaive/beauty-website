Who We Are

We were awarded and voted the best college of the year 2016. We commenced training 17 years ago with a vision to equip our students with the highest international stardards and skills-sets in Cosmetology. Cosmetology offers endless careers options from celeblity styling, hair colourist, estheticians, nail technicians, make up artists, elecrologists, spa therapist, spa owners, tutor, just to mention a few. We own and manage 13 beauty centers where we guarantee jobs at your outlets to the top graduates, while offering career services to the other graduates at other salons, spa's and Beauty centers.



Objectives

To revolutionize the beauty industry in terms of professionalism by making hair dressing and beauty a profession of choice not a last resort and empower the youth by creating job opportunities as we serve throughout kenya, regionally and internationally.
